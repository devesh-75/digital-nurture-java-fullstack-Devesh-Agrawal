Airtel Products and Services Overview:

Based on the Airtel website (https://www.airtel.in/), the main products and services offered include:

1. Mobile Services (Prepaid & Postpaid):
   - Prepaid plans (unlimited calls, data packs, roaming, entertainment bundles like Disney+ Hotstar, Amazon Prime).
   - Postpaid plans (Family plans, data rollover, Airtel Black integrations).
   - International Roaming packs.
   - eSIM services.

2. Broadband & Fiber:
   - Airtel Xstream Fiber (high-speed broadband up to 1 Gbps, unlimited data, voice calls).
   - Bundled Wi-Fi routers.

3. DTH (Direct-to-Home) TV:
   - Airtel Xstream Play (Smart Android TV Box, 4K picture quality, Google Assistant).
   - Standard HD and SD set-top boxes with custom channels and packs.

4. Financial Services:
   - Airtel Payments Bank (Savings account, digital wallet, UPI payments, utility bill payments, recharges, insurance).

5. Enterprise & Business Solutions (Airtel Business):
   - Connectivity (Leased lines, MPLS, SD-WAN).
   - Cloud and Data Center services.
   - IoT (Internet of Things) and cybersecurity solutions.
   - CPaaS (Airtel IQ - SMS, Voice, WhatsApp API).

6. Airtel Black:
   - Customized combination plans pooling Mobile, Fiber, and DTH connections under a single bill with premium customer support.
